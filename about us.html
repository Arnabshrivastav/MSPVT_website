About Management

It is governend by Anishka Educational Society of Kota which is run by qulaified engineers of various streams. The president of society is an eminent personality of society Ms. Deepti Manoj Singh a great scholar. The secretary is Mr. Mahendra Kumar Verma, a production engineer from Government College, Kota and a well known educationalist. The treasurer is Mr. Nitin Verma a computer engineer from Government College, Kota and a great teacher. Their thoughts to do something for the society and common person gave rise to the ANISHKA EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY, dedicated for technical education for each and every person at no profit no loss concept.


About Principal

"Technical education is the key to closed doors of employment."
In order to implement this message, Maa Saraswati Pvt ITI takes full responsibility of the personality development and skill development of the students, which will help the students to get regular education and training. College is running successfully under the guidance of Er. Randeep Joshi.


About College

Maa Saraswati Pvt ITI is located in Kota in the state of Rajasthan. It was established in 2014 by Anishka Educational Society, Kota to enhance the technical education in the state. The college has been established in the heart of Kota, Shrinath Puram Area about 12 kms from Kota Railway Station and 10 kms from Kota Bus Stand and just nearby Rajasthan Technical University-Kota


Our Objective

To train the youth into a skilled worker and to help him/her in employment/self-employment and open new ventures. To provide skilled technician having latest know-how of the latest equipments according to the need of industry & establishment in public and private sector.


Our Mission

This institute strives hard to achieve excellence in technical education & industrial training to maintain our number one position in the state. We believe in four cardinal points of achievements:
  1. To equip the students with latest emerging technologies, skills and knowledge up to the level of globally competitive standards.
  2. To maintain a continuous interface with industry and to bring changes with time according to the needs of industry.
  3. To enhance the ability to anticipate and shape further the skills of budding engineers though individual and group activities to include and encourage a life long thirst for learning in students.
  4. To encourage the conceptual, inter personnel and team work skills for technical decision making among the students.
